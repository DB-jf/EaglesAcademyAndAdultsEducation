About Us

Eagles Academy offers quality primary and secondary education alongside adult education programmes designed to empower learners of all ages.

Our Mission

To provide an inclusive, high-quality education that develops confident, responsible and motivated learners.

Our Vision

To be a centre of academic excellence and community leadership.

Core Values

Integrity, Discipline, Innovation, Respect and Service.

Academics

A balanced curriculum with a strong focus on STEM, languages, arts and practical skills.

Primary School

Strong fundamentals, literacy and numeracy-focused programmes with activities and clubs.

Secondary School

Comprehensive subject offerings, exam preparation and career guidance.

Adult Education

Flexible evening and weekend classes for adults seeking certifications or skill upgrades.

Facilities

  • Modern library and learning resource centre
  • Fully equipped science and computer laboratories
  • Sports field and indoor sports hall
  • Secure boarding/hostel facilities (where applicable)
