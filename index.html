    About Us

    Eagles Academy offers quality primary and secondary education alongside adult education programmes designed to empower learners of all ages.

    Our Mission

    To provide an inclusive, high-quality education that develops confident, responsible and motivated learners.

    Our Vision

    To be a centre of academic excellence and community leadership.

    Core Values

    Integrity, Discipline, Innovation, Respect and Service.

    Academics

    A balanced curriculum with a strong focus on STEM, languages, arts and practical skills.

    Primary School

    Strong fundamentals, literacy and numeracy-focused programmes with activities and clubs.

    Secondary School

    Comprehensive subject offerings, exam preparation and career guidance.

    Adult Education

    Flexible evening and weekend classes for adults seeking certifications or skill upgrades.

    Facilities

    Modern Library

    Our library and learning resource centre is equipped with a wide range of books, digital resources, and study spaces for students to excel academically.

    Science & Computer Labs

    Fully equipped science and computer laboratories provide hands-on experience, fostering innovation and discovery in a modern learning environment.

    Sports Facilities

    From our large sports field to the indoor sports hall, students can enjoy physical activities and team sports in well-maintained facilities.

    Boarding/Hostel

    Our secure and comfortable boarding facilities provide a supportive environment for students living on campus.
